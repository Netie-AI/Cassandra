Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-26 · Cassandra by Netie AI

Cassandra (crash.netie.ai) is a market research desk operated by Netie AI. This policy describes what we collect, why, and your choices.

Data we collect

  • Email address (account sign-up, free digest, or contact form)
  • Subscription tier and payment status (via Stripe; we do not store card numbers)
  • Usage logs (API requests, agent chat gate events, pipeline diagnostics)
  • Browser/device metadata when you submit a contact form (for support only)

What we do not do

We do not sell your data. We do not use third-party advertising trackers. We do not execute trades on your behalf.

Where data is stored

  • Accounts and profiles: Supabase (EU region when configured)
  • Public score snapshots: Cloudflare KV
  • Pipeline history: local SQLite on the operator machine (not exported to third parties)
  • Email delivery: Resend (send-only)
  • Payments: Stripe (PCI-compliant; Netie AI never sees full card data)

Email

We send edition digests, account messages, and support replies from Netie AI addresses. Every marketing-style email includes an unsubscribe link. Reply to hello@netie.ai for privacy questions.

Your rights

  • Unsubscribe from the free digest anytime via the link in any email
  • Delete your account by contacting hello@netie.ai
  • Request a copy or correction of data we hold about you
